Privacy Policy

The Feedme application is a platform for buying and selling pet food. It requires user registration and login. The application serves both customers and business operators, including delivery services within the vicinity of stores. By using the application, users agree to the collection, use, and disclosure of information as specified in this Privacy Policy.

2. Collection of Personal Information

We collect user information as necessary to provide services within the application, including:

  • Customers: Personal information such as full name, phone number, email, address, and current location (if pinned for delivery).
  • Business Operators: Store information, product listings, application updates, and the location of customers who place orders.

3. Use of Information

The information we collect will be used to:

  • Manage user accounts and login processes.
  • Provide product search and payment systems.
  • Handle orders, including selecting delivery methods.
  • Facilitate product delivery using the customer's current location.
  • Allow business operators to add, edit, and delete product listings.
  • Generate daily and monthly sales reports.

4. Information Sharing

Personal information of users will not be disclosed to third parties except in cases where:

  • It is necessary to disclose to third parties to provide services, such as delivery companies or payment service providers.
  • It complies with legal requirements or court orders.

5. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate safeguards to protect users' personal information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

6. User Rights

Users have the right to access, modify, or request the deletion of their personal information through the application settings or by contacting our support team.
